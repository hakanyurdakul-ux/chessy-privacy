Privacy Policy

BitSize Games ("we", "us") built Chessy as a free, ad-supported game. This page informs you of our policies regarding data when you use our app.

Data Collection

Chessy does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection for gameplay. No account creation is needed.

Advertising

Chessy displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

• Device identifiers (advertising ID)
• General location data (country-level)
• App usage data (for ad frequency and relevance)

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, Chessy will request your permission before allowing ad networks to track your activity across other apps and websites. You may decline this request and still use the app normally.

Children's Privacy

Chessy is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

• Google AdMob — for serving advertisements

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
